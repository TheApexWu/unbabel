Q&A CHEAT SHEET:

"Why not WhatsApp?"
-> WhatsApp groups are walled by language. Bengali group and
   Mexican group in the same building never cross. We're
   the only place their signals merge.

"How do you get users?"
-> One church, one legal clinic. They already have multilingual
   foot traffic and paper bulletin boards. We digitize what's
   already happening.

"What about fake reports?"
-> You can't fake cross-language corroboration. One phone = one
   alias. You'd need multiple SIMs and fluency in multiple
   languages.

"Isn't this Reddit?"
-> Reddit aggregates opinions. We aggregate evidence. No feed,
   no votes, no algorithm. This is epidemiology, not social media.

"Who pays?"
-> City agencies subscribe to the signal feed. Tenant advocacy
   orgs pay for real-time alerts. We never charge immigrants.

"PII / safety?"
-> Phone hashed client-side, never stored. PII stripped from
   post text. Posts expire in 7 days. No accounts, no login.

"What does immigrant-aware moderation mean?"
-> "ICE was at the subway" = safety warning, not threat. We
   mark it SAFE. Standard moderation would flag it. "Cash job?"
   = survival, not fraud. Only direct threats/doxxing = UNSAFE.
