Q&A (keep short, don't ramble)

"Isn't this Reddit?"
-> Reddit aggregates opinions. We aggregate evidence.
   Epidemiology, not social media.

"Why not WhatsApp?"
-> Walled by language. Bengali and Mexican groups
   never cross. We merge the signals.

"How do you get users?"
-> One church, one legal clinic. They already have
   multilingual foot traffic. We digitize paper
   bulletin boards.

"Fake reports?"
-> Can't fake cross-language corroboration.
   One phone = one alias. Need multiple SIMs
   AND multiple languages to game it.

"Who pays?"
-> City agencies, tenant orgs, advocacy groups.
   Never charge immigrants.

"Liability?"
-> Section 230. Signals state facts about reports,
   not claims about entities. PII stripped. 7-day expiry.

"Technical moat?"
-> Single-inference multi-task pipeline. Cross-lingual
   entity resolution. The AI is infrastructure, not
   the product. Epidemiological pattern detection
   across language boundaries.

"Learning to be American?"
-> Being American isn't learning English. It's learning
   that your voice combines with strangers' voices to
   create power.
